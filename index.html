Scrambled Eggs

Author: Amy

Published: March 12, 2019

Scrambled Eggs Source

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup half-n-half
  • 1/4 teaspoon Kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • black pepper, for serving
  • fresh chopped herbs, for serving

Recipe instructions

1. Use the Mixing Bowl and Whisk
Whisking can gets rid of any streak of folk and whites.
2. Use Whole Milk, Half-n-Half,Cream, or Mayonnaise
This will adds creaminess to the body and depth flavor. Make use to use the butter on the pan. This will help with the egg moving around without sticking to the pan.
3. Use the Nonstick Sturdy Pan
This will help with the eggs gliding around the pan without sticking.
4. Use the Rubber Spatula
This will give flexibility. It can hug the curve of your pan.
5. Heat
Start on medium heat to melt the butter. When you added the eggs make sure to reduce the heat to medium-low. if your pan is too hot, it can cause the eggs to cook fast and it will be brown and dry out.
6. Wait for a Few Seconds
Do this depend on your preference.If you want beautiful soft curds, wait. If not then your curds will be smaller.
7. Don't Overcook your Eggs
You want your eggs to be little of wett so that it can look fluffy
