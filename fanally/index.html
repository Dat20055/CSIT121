About Jeonghyun Seok

Jeonghyun Seok is a South Korean illustrator who has published a 660-page book titled "Stonehouse's Anatomy." The book provides lessons on anatomy, as its name suggests. Seok also works as an artist for MARVEL and has been awarded the Korea Manhwa content award in 2006, as well as the SPP Webtoon award in 2018.
